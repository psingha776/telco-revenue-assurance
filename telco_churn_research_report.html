10. Plan Feature Gap Analysis

Usage vs Plan Allowance (High-Risk Customers)

Root Cause Identified: High-risk customers are on voice-centric plans but have data-centric behavior. They use 154% of their 1GB data cap but only 13% of their 200 voice minutes. The plan shape is wrong for their usage pattern, generating surprise overages that trigger complaints that trigger churn.
